Hunt for shots—not controls

Keep your eyes on your subject with easy-to-operate exposure, ISO, and white balance controls, intuitively placed around the shutter button.

Find your focus fast

Freely focus anywhere just by touching the rear monitor—use Full-Area Focusing to key in on any part of your photo or Pinpoint AF for detailed adjustments. In manual focus, you can enlarge your monitor view up to 10X.

Speedy image delivery
